# Tech Stack Logos

Place technology/framework logos here.

## Naming Convention

Use lowercase tech names (SVG preferred, PNG also works):

- `swift.svg` or `swift.png`
- `react.svg` or `react.png`
- `firebase.svg` or `firebase.png`
- `nodejs.svg` or `nodejs.png`
- `tailwind.svg` or `tailwind.png`
- `nextjs.svg` or `nextjs.png`
- `flutter.svg` or `flutter.png`
- `supabase.svg` or `supabase.png`
- `vercel.svg` or `vercel.png`
- `testflight.svg` or `testflight.png`
- `bluetooth.svg` or `bluetooth.png`

## Recommended Specs

- Format: PNG with transparent background or SVG
- Size: Square, 128×128px or 256×256px
- File size: Keep under 50KB
